SAUTI SOL

My favorite band is one well known to many Kenyans and probably one of the most renowned bands in Africa. Apparently this is an all-boys-band hey go by the name Sauti Sol.

Group Bio

The group was formed in Nairobi by Bien, Willis and Savara in 2005 then as an Acappela but would later be joined by guitarist Polycarp. The boys met in Upper Hill school while undertaking their o-levels- then performing in school's drama and music festivals. They would later meet and formed Sauti- Sauti swahili for 'Sound'. The would later add 'Sol' spanish for sun, to mean Sound in the Sun. They scripted their first son 'Mafunzo' that was later featured in their first album Mwanzo swahili for 'Start'. Sauti Sol have since toured Africa, the US and Europe in their concerts all over the world.
